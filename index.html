Description

Par Elizabeth le 5/02/2019 à 09:00

At the end of time we struggle to survive by facing all kinds of dangers. Caster has an awakening from the Almighty God; there is a solution to save the world, Excalibur.
The mistic sword is jealously guarded by monsters of all time. They attack helpless people in their dreams and steal their minds.
Our heroes will try to save us from the apocalypse. Their purpose is to fight monsters. They have to find Excalibur to prevent the end of time.

Characters

Heroes

The Warrior is the best fighter with overwhelming skills in offence.

The Paladin is the champion of the Realm with the holy armour.

The Caster is the princess of mages with the knowledge of the elders.

Monsters

Packy is the soul eater motivated by kills and destructions.

Slime ithe stickiest of monsters, prince of the deceitful.

The Dragon is the most powerful fire eater, guardian of Excalibur.

The Bat is the monster of shadow, fed by the darkness of souls.




Rules of game

The game is simple and easy to learn. It’s a single player game. There are three battle levels.
To change levels, we must kill all monsters in the dungeon room. We have to move forward in the levels to have a chance to find Excalibur.
The winner of the match will receive a trophy.
The better player will be rewarded by hall of fame.
